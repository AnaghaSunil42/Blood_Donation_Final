About Lifeline

Lifeline is a critical infrastructure project aimed at solving blood scarcity and aligning with the UN's Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Well-Being.

Our centralized platform bridges the gap between generous donors, patients in need, and medical facilities. By leveraging technology like real-time mapping and AI-assisted support, we ensure that every critical drop of blood finds its way to save a life, faster and more efficiently than ever before.
